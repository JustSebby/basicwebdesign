Five Nights At Freddy's

In Five Nights at Freddy's you are playing as a night security guard by the name of Mike Schmidt working at a family pizza restaurant named Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your goal is to survive until 6am while avoiding 4 animatronics. Freddy Fazbear,Bonnie The Bunny, Chica The Chicken and Foxy The Pirate Fox. At the start of your night shifts you get a daily call to instruct you on the nights. You have access to cameras to know the animatronics movement. You have access to doors to let the animatronics out of your office to jumpscare you. If you don't do so then it'll result in one of the animatronics jumpscaring you and in a game over screen where there's a freddy suit sitting on the parts in service table with your eyes bulged out of the suit.

Chararcters

The Chararcters include of Ralph aka Phone Guy,Mike Schmidt the nightguard which is you the player,Freddy Fazbear is the leader of the band,Bonnie the Bunny is a purple bunny who's the guitarist of the band. Chica The Chicken is the lady of the group and the backup singer, Foxy The Pirate Fox is a eyed fox with a hook for his right hand and last but not least Golden Freddy which is a yellow version of Freddy.

Random Facts

  • Scott Cawthon (The Creator of FNaF) conceived the idea of FNaF based on criticism of his previous game, Chipper and Sons Lumber Co. Reviewers complained on frightening chararcters that had animatronic-like movement which inspired Scott Cawthon to create a scarier experience.
  • The Concept of FNaF is simiar to a 2006 comedy film Night at the Museum.
  • Scott Cawthon was scared of Bonnie as he had plenty of nightmares when developing FNaF 1.
  • On rare occasions when starting up the game or after the game over screen there's a 0.1% of Bonnie with white pupils.
